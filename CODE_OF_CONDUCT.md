# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in HyperGPU welcoming and respectful for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent while being clear about risks.
- Be careful with security-sensitive guidance.
- Respect maintainer decisions and project scope.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Encouraging unsafe use of privileged scripts or systems.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject contributions; or restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and productive.
